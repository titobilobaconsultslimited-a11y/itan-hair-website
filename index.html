Our Story

Born From
a Passion
for Hair

Itan Academy was founded with a singular mission — to elevate the standard of hair artistry in Nigeria and across Africa. What started as a small salon has grown into a movement, training hundreds of stylists and serving thousands of clients who trust us with their most prized possession.

✂️
What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01
Excellence Always
We hold every strand to the highest standard. Whether it's a simple trim or a complex colour, excellence is non-negotiable at Itan.
02
Celebrating Heritage
African hair is magnificent. We honour every texture, pattern and style — from natural locs to bold weaves — with equal expertise and pride.
03
Empowering Careers
We believe every person deserves the tools to build a thriving career. Our academy programmes are designed to transform talent into livelihoods.
04
Community First
Itan is more than a business — it's a community. We invest in our clients, our students and the neighbourhoods we serve.
